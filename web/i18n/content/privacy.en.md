## 1. Overview

The Lucid Learning Co., Ltd. (doing business as Headquarter.ai, "we," "us," or "our") recognizes the critical importance of data privacy to our customers and is committed to collecting, processing, and protecting personal data in a transparent and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how we collect, use, store, and protect customer information when customers use the Headquarter.ai platform and related services (collectively, the "Services"), and explains customers' rights with respect to their data.

## 2. Types of Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

1. **Information You Provide to Us**:  Data required for account registration, service activation, and contractual purposes, including company name, contact person name, job title, email address, phone number, company address, and business registration number.
2. **Information We Collect Automatically**: Including but not limited to service usage logs (login timestamps, activity records, API call logs) and device and connection information (IP address, browser type, operating system). This data is used solely for service operations and security purposes and is not used for any other purpose.
3. **Customer Content**: Content that customers upload, submit, or generate during use of the Services ("Customer Content"), including but not limited to documents and data uploaded by customers and content processed by us in building knowledge bases on customers' behalf. We will only access Customer Content in the following circumstances:
   1. To build, maintain, or improve knowledge bases and AI Agent functionality based on customer requirements.
   2. To provide technical support based on customer needs.
   3. As required by law.

## 3. Use of Data

We collect customer data solely for the following purposes:

1. **Service Delivery and Operations**: Account creation and management, technical support, payment processing, and fulfillment of contractual obligations.
2. **Service Improvement**: Using de-identified or anonymized data to analyze usage patterns, improve features, and enhance the user experience.
3. **Security**: Detecting and preventing fraud, unauthorized access, and cybersecurity threats.
4. **Legal Compliance**: Compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

## 4. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
| --- | --- |
| Account Data | Per customer instruction, or within 30 days of contract termination |
| Service Usage Logs | Per customer instruction, or within 30 days of contract termination |
| Customer Content | Per customer instruction, or within 30 days of contract termination |
| Customer Service Records | 2 years |
| Transaction and Contract Records | 10 years |

Upon expiration of the retention period, we will delete the relevant data by default.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell customer personal data. We may disclose customer data only in the following circumstances:

1. **Cloud Infrastructure Provider**: The Services are built on Amazon Web Services (AWS). Servers may be located in Taiwan, the Asia-Pacific region, or the United States. Data processing is governed by AWS's standard terms of service and data processing addendum. For details, please refer to the [AWS Privacy Policy](https://aws.amazon.com/privacy).
2. **Legal Requirements**: If a competent authority requires disclosure of customer data under applicable law, we will notify the customer prior to disclosure unless prohibited by law, and will disclose only to the extent required.
3. **Corporate Transactions:**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer involving Customer data, we will notify customers prior to the transfer.

## 6. Data Storage and Security

**Storage Locations** Data for the Services is stored on cloud infrastructure located in Taiwan, the Asia-Pacific region, or the United States, depending on the services, features, and customer requirements. Customer data may be transferred to and processed in these regions. We ensure that relevant cloud service providers maintain security measures consistent with international standards.

**Security Measures**
- We use industry-standard encryption to protect data in transit and at rest.
- We enforce strict access controls and the principle of least privilege.
- We conduct regular vulnerability scanning and penetration testing.
- All personnel are subject to security training and confidentiality obligations.
- We maintain established incident response and breach management procedures.


## 7. Your Rights

Customers have the following rights with respect to their personal data held by the Company:

1. Right to inquire or request access
2. Right to request a copy of data
3. Right to request correction or supplementation
4. Right to restrict or object to the processing of your data
5. Right to request deletion

To exercise any of the above rights, please submit a written or email request to us. We may require the requester to provide necessary identity verification information and will respond within 15 business days of receipt.

## 8. Customer Content

1. Customers retain all rights to the input content they provide to the Services and the output generated by the Services. The Company acquires only a limited license to Customer Content as necessary to provide the Services.
2. We will not use Customer Content to train any AI models without the customer's prior written consent.
3. Our access to Customer Content is limited to the following circumstances:
   1. Building, maintaining, or improving knowledge bases and AI Agent functionality based on customer requirements.
   2. Providing technical support based on customer needs.
   3. As required by law.
4. All personnel with access to Customer Content are bound by confidentiality obligations.
5. Customer data may include personal data of minors depending on the nature of the customer's business. We handle such data in accordance with the principles above. Customers bear full responsibility for ensuring that personal data they provide has been lawfully collected and used.
6. If a competent authority requests Customer Content, we will make reasonable efforts to notify the customer in advance so that they may seek appropriate legal remedies.
